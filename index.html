GasTrack

Problem: Finding the right gas bottle can be a frustrating experience. You might spend time calling or visiting stores, only to discover they don't have the specific gas you need. Solution: We're building GasTrack, a user-friendly web application that will connect you with nearby gas stores and their real-time gas inventory. Simply enter the type of gas bottle you need, and GasTrack will show you the closest stores that have it in stock.

  • Save time and effort: No more wasted trips or endless phone calls!
  • Convenience: Find what you need quickly and easily from the comfort of your home.
  • Transparency: Real-time inventory ensures you only visit stores that have your gas in stock.

About Us

About Campustech Solutions

Founded in 2023, Campustech Solutions has been at the forefront of technological advancement in Cameroon. We are committed to bridging the digital divide and fostering innovation across the nation.

Our team of passionate tech enthusiasts and educators works tirelessly to provide cutting-edge solutions, comprehensive training programs, and opportunities for both aspiring and established tech professionals.

Our Mission

To empower Cameroonians with technological skills, provide innovative solutions, and promote gender equality in the tech sector, driving the country's digital transformation

Our Vision

A technologically advanced Cameroon where every citizen, regardless of gender, has the opportunity to thrive in the digital economy.
